For a long time, the Meteor community has discussed pnpm support and the limitations of the legacy Meteor bundler around symlinks, workspace layouts, and dependency resolution. With Meteor 3.4+ and the move into the modern build stack through Rspack integration, many of these limitations are no longer blockers.
